Design a modern, responsive, and visually engaging landing website UI for “Serveme.lk” — an all-in-one restaurant management and customer engagement platform. The design should have a clean SaaS-style interface mixed with modern food delivery aesthetics similar to Uber Eats, Toast POS, and OpenTable.

The website should target:

1. Restaurant owners/businesses
2. Food lovers/customers

Use a premium UI style with:

* Modern typography
* Soft gradients
* Glassmorphism/light card shadows
* Rounded corners
* Professional food imagery
* Warm and appetizing color palette (orange, red, dark charcoal, white)
* Mobile-first responsive design
* Smooth scrolling sections
* Modern animations and hover effects

---
